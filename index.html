Advanced Skin Rejuvenation & Treatment :

Healthy, radiant skin is the foundation of confidence and self-care. Our advanced skin rejuvenation treatments are designed to restore your skin’s youthful glow while addressing a variety of concerns, including wrinkles, fine lines, acne scars, and uneven skin texture. Using the latest technology and proven techniques, we ensure safe, effective, and lasting results for all skin types.

Aging, environmental factors, and lifestyle choices can cause visible signs of skin damage over time. Our tailored treatments help reduce the appearance of wrinkles and fine lines, leaving your skin smoother, firmer, and revitalized. With a focus on natural-looking results, our experts carefully evaluate your skin to create a customized plan that enhances your individual beauty.

Neck Rejuvenation :

Neck rejuvenation is a specialized aesthetic treatment designed to restore firmness, smoothness, and youthful definition to the delicate neck area. Over time, factors such as aging, sun exposure, and lifestyle habits can lead to sagging skin, wrinkles, and visible banding. Neck rejuvenation addresses these concerns by combining advanced techniques that tighten, lift, and improve overall skin texture for a more refined appearance.

Modern neck rejuvenation treatments offer a range of solutions tailored to each individual’s needs. Options such as laser resurfacing, injectable fillers, Botox for platysmal bands, microneedling, and energy-based skin tightening work together to stimulate collagen, smooth creases, and redefine contours. These minimally invasive procedures provide noticeable improvements with little downtime, making them ideal for patients seeking natural, long-lasting results and comfort.

Lip fillers :

Lip fillers are a popular aesthetic treatment designed to enhance lip volume, improve symmetry, and create a naturally fuller appearance. Using safe, hyaluronic acid–based injectables, dermatologists shape the lips with precision to achieve soft, balanced, and youthful results.

The procedure is quick, minimally invasive, and offers instant improvement with little to no downtime. Whether the goal is subtle definition, hydration, or a more noticeable enhancement, lip fillers can be tailored to suit each individual’s facial proportions and aesthetic preferences.

Skin lightening treatments :

Skin lightening treatments focus on reducing pigmentation, evening out skin tone, and restoring a brighter, more radiant complexion. These procedures target concerns like dark spots, melasma, tanning, acne marks, and uneven discoloration caused by sun exposure or hormonal changes.

Dermatologists use a combination of advanced therapies such as chemical peels, laser treatments, glutathione, mesotherapy, and medical-grade topical products to safely lighten and clarify the skin. Each treatment plan is customized based on skin type and sensitivity to ensure effective results without compromising skin health.

Cutting-Edge Aesthetic Face Care :

Cutting-edge aesthetic face care blends advanced dermatological techniques with innovative technologies to rejuvenate, sculpt, and refine the skin. These treatments focus on restoring youthful radiance while preserving the natural contours of the face, ensuring results that look fresh rather than artificial.

Modern procedures such as fillers, Botox, lasers, and microneedling address wrinkles, pigmentation, uneven texture, and volume loss with precision. Each treatment is customized to meet individual skin needs, helping patients achieve smoother, brighter, and more balanced facial features with minimal downtime.

Meet Dr. Neda Adibi

Dr. Neda Adibi is a distinguished Specialist Dermatologist at Dr. Sulaiman Al Habib Medical Centre, Dubai. A top graduate of Isfahan University of Medical Sciences, she earned her Dermatology Board Certification in 2006 and later served as faculty and Deputy Editor-in-Chief of the Journal of Research in Medical Sciences. A pioneer in psychodermatology, she founded the first clinic of its kind at IUMS. She offers advanced aesthetic and medical dermatology treatments including fillers, Botox, lasers, PRP, surgical corrections, acne, vitiligo, and hair loss management. With 30+ research publications, she blends scientific expertise with compassionate care. Dr. Neda is deeply committed to enhancing patient confidence, delivering natural results through evidence-based treatments tailored uniquely to every individual. Her practice focuses on long-term skin health, combining innovative technologies with a patient-first approach to achieve safe, refined, and lasting outcomes.
